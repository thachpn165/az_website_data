## Frequently asked questions
Does AZDIGI design e-commerce websites? __

AZDIGI is a hosting and VPS infrastructure provider. We don't design or develop e-commerce websites, but provide powerful infrastructure for you or your development team to deploy online stores.

Does an online store need hosting or VPS? __

Depends on scale: Small stores (<500 products) can use Business Hosting. Large stores with high traffic or customization needs should use VPS for full admin access.

Does AZDIGI support SSL for payments? __

Yes, all hosting plans include free Let's Encrypt SSL. We also support EV SSL for higher security if needed.

How about backup and security? __

AZDIGI auto backups daily, 30-day retention. System includes Firewall, Anti-DDoS and free SSL to protect your online store.
