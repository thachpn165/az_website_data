## Frequently asked questions
Which VPS is suitable for Odoo? __

Odoo needs at least 4GB RAM for 1-5 users. Recommend VPS from 8GB RAM for 10-20 users team, 16GB+ for larger teams.

Is Odoo Community feature-complete? __

Odoo Community has all basic modules: Accounting, Inventory, Sales, Purchase. Enterprise only adds some advanced features.

Can ERP integrate with WooCommerce? __

Yes, Odoo and ERPNext have connectors to sync with WooCommerce, Magento for inventory and order management.

Does AZDIGI support ERP installation? __

Yes, we support free Odoo, ERPNext installation. Can also support basic configuration on request.
