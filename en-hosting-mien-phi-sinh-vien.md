I'm not a student, can I register? __

Yes. You can still register for free hosting with a 3-month cycle. Only students with ID cards get 6 months.

Does free hosting have ads? __

No. AZDIGI Free Hosting has absolutely no ads. Your website is clean like paid packages.

Can I renew the free package? __

Possible, but need to contact sales department. Free package doesn't auto-renew. Recommend upgrading to Pro Hosting for better experience.

Is 1GB storage enough? __

1GB is enough for basic WordPress website, personal blog or portfolio. If you need more, upgrade to Pro Hosting from $3.5/month.

Can I use my own domain? __

Free package only supports free subdomains (azdigi.blog, azdigi.page, azdigi.shop). To use your own domain, you need to upgrade to paid package.

How long is activation time? __

After submitting complete verification info, sales department will activate within 1-3 business hours.

Is WordPress supported? __

Yes. AZDIGI Free Hosting is optimized for WordPress with LiteSpeed Cache. You also get free themes and plugins worth $1,600+.

What if I violate the rules? __

AZDIGI will permanently lock the hosting if prohibited content is detected. Please read the terms carefully before registering.
