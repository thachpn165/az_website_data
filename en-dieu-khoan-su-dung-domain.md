## Usage Principles

Domain names chosen by organizations or individuals for registration must ensure:

  * No phrases that harm national interests
  * Compliant with social ethics and national customs
  * Demonstrate seriousness to avoid misunderstanding or distortion due to homonyms, multiple meanings, or when Vietnamese diacritics are not used



 _Reference: Point 2.2a, Clause 2, Section II of Circular 24/2015/TT-BTTTT on Internet resource management and use._

## Domain Registration

AZDIGI provides international and Vietnamese domain registration:

  * International domains: .com, .net, .org, .info, .biz...
  * Vietnamese domains: .vn, .com.vn, .net.vn...
  * New domains: .shop, .online, .store, .tech...



### Registration requirements:

  * Accurate, complete registrant information
  * ID card or Business License (for .vn domains)
  * Active confirmation email



3 

## Domain Renewal

Domain renewal regulations:

  * Renew at least 7 days before expiration
  * After expiration, domain enters Grace Period (30-45 days)
  * After Grace Period, domain enters Redemption (high fee)
  * After Redemption, domain is released



**Note:** AZDIGI is not responsible for domain loss due to non-renewal.

4 

## Domain Transfer

### Transfer In to AZDIGI

  * Domain must be registered for over 60 days
  * Unlock domain at current registrar
  * Get EPP/Authorization Code
  * Confirm transfer email



### Transfer Out

  * Request unlock via support ticket
  * Receive EPP code via email
  * Free transfer out

## Domain Disputes

In case of domain disputes:

  * AZDIGI follows ICANN's UDRP policy
  * .vn domains follow VNNIC regulations
  * Customers bear legal responsibility for trademark-infringing registrations



AZDIGI may suspend domains upon request from authorities.

6 

## WHOIS Policy

Domain WHOIS information:

  * Public information per ICANN regulations
  * WHOIS Privacy available for international domains
  * .vn domains: public information per VNNIC regulations



**WHOIS Privacy:** Hides personal information, replaced with AZDIGI's info. Additional fee may apply.

  * Decree 27/2018/ND-CP dated 01/03/2018 by the Government amending Decree 72/2013/ND-CP on management, provision, and use of Internet services and online information
