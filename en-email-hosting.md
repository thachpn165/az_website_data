## Frequently Asked Questions
Can Email Hosting be used with existing domain? __

Yes. You can use Email Hosting with any registered domain. Just point MX record to AZDIGI server.

Is email configuration on phone supported? __

Yes. AZDIGI provides detailed guides and technical support for email configuration on iOS, Android, Outlook, Thunderbird and other email apps.

Will emails go to spam when sending? __

AZDIGI configures SPF, DKIM, DMARC fully for high email reliability. However, email content also affects spam filtering.

Can I add more mailboxes later? __

Yes. You can upgrade plan or purchase additional individual mailboxes anytime.

Is Email Hosting backed up? __

Yes. Email is backed up automatically daily. You can request email restoration from backup if needed.

Is email migration from other hosting free? __

Yes. AZDIGI supports free email migration from old hosting. Technical team will assist the entire transition process.
