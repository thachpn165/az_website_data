## Commission Calculation

AZDIGI applies a **fixed 10% commission** on order value for:

  * New registration orders
  * Service renewal orders



Commission applies to both new and existing customers referred through affiliate links.

**Note:** Some services may have higher commission rates during special promotional programs. AZDIGI will notify in advance when applying these programs.

2 

## Service Promotion

AZDIGI allows affiliates to use all channels to promote our brand, images, and services to effectively reach users.

However, AZDIGI **does not allow** affiliates to use the keyword "AZDIGI" (or similar variations) for paid advertising services (Facebook Ads, Google Adwords, etc.) as this is a protected trademark exclusively used for internal company purposes.

3 

## Pending Commission

Affiliates can only withdraw from commissions that have been approved to "Current Commission" status.

When a partner successfully refers a customer:

  * Commission will be labeled as "Pending Commission"
  * Holding period: **40 days**
  * After 40 days, if the customer continues using the service → pending commission becomes withdrawable
  * Commission will be cancelled if customer cancels the service



4 

## Commission Withdrawal

Once the withdrawable balance in the affiliate account reaches **1,000,000 VND** or more, partners can withdraw by clicking the payment request button on the affiliate page.

  * **Payment method:** Bank transfer
  * **Processing time:** 1-3 business days



5 

## Personal Income Tax

All partners withdrawing from AZDIGI are responsible for paying **10% Personal Income Tax** as required by law when monthly income equals or exceeds **2,000,000 VND**.

  * AZDIGI will deduct directly from the withdrawal amount to pay on behalf of the partner
  * If partner withdraws less than 2,000,000 VND: deduction will be applied to the next withdrawal in the month based on total withdrawn amount



6 

## Affiliate Contract

For the first withdrawal, we will require partners to provide:

  * Copy of ID card/Citizen ID
  * Sign affiliate contract using AZDIGI's template



**Note:** Each partner only needs to submit this information once.
