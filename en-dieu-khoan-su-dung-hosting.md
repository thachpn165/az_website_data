## Unlimited Hosting Service Limits
Regulations for Unlimited Hosting plans:

  * Web Hosting must not be used as storage or backup repository
  * Accounts using excessive or unreasonable storage will be requested to reduce usage
  * AZDIGI reserves the right to suspend or terminate service for storage resource abuse

## Email Limits
Web Hosting is designed for website storage and operation, so the included email feature may not be optimized for email purposes. AZDIGI does not guarantee that Email on Web Hosting will work accurately and deliver stably. For frequent email needs, customers should use the Email Hosting service.

Additionally, we have the following email sending limits:

  * Maximum **100 emails/hour/domain**
  * Bounce rate cannot exceed **30%** of total hourly allowed emails
  * Email queue cannot exceed **100%** of total hourly allowed emails

## Prohibited Content
The following content is **not allowed** on hosting:

  * Content violating Vietnamese law
  * Malware, viruses, malicious code
  * Copyright infringing content
  * Phishing, scam websites
  * Pornographic content
  * Proxy, VPN servers
  * Cryptocurrency mining
  * Spam email sending

Violations will result in immediate account suspension without refund.
