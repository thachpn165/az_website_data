## Premium Business Hosting FAQ

What type of website is Premium Business Hosting suitable for? __

Premium Business Hosting is suitable for SMB websites, e-commerce websites, professional blogs, landing pages and projects requiring stability, high security with medium to high traffic.

How many websites can I host on one Premium Business package? __

Depends on your plan: Business Starter for 3 websites, Business Pro for 10 websites, Business Enterprise unlimited. Total resource usage must be within package limits.

Can I upgrade from regular hosting to Premium Business? __

Yes, you can upgrade anytime. We support free data migration and charge the difference based on remaining time.

How is backup performed? __

The system automatically backs up all data daily and stores for 30 days. You can restore free via JetBackup in cPanel or request support from technical team.

Do I get free website migration support? __

Yes, we support free migration of up to 3 websites from other hosting providers. Technical team will ensure smooth transition.

What is the refund policy? __

We provide 100% refund policy within the first 30 days if you're not satisfied, no reason needed.

How is Premium Business different from Pro Platinum Hosting? __

Premium Business Hosting focuses on stability and resource control for businesses with higher dedicated CPU/RAM. Pro Platinum optimizes for performance at budget price, suitable for individuals and small projects.

Is SSL installation supported? __

Yes, Let's Encrypt SSL is automatically installed free for all domains. You can also install paid SSL if needed.
