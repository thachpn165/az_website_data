## Frequently Asked Questions
What does Unlimited mean? __

Unlimited means you're not restricted by hard limits on storage or bandwidth. However, Fair Use policy applies - for normal websites, not file sharing or backup storage.

Can I upload videos? __

Yes, you can upload videos for your website. However, for large video streaming needs, we recommend dedicated CDN like YouTube, Vimeo or Bunny CDN.

What is Fair Use policy? __

Fair Use ensures resources are shared fairly. Unlimited is for normal websites, not for: file sharing, backup storage, streaming servers, or CPU-intensive applications.

Is there a file (inode) limit? __

No hard limit on files. However, to ensure system performance, inodes are managed at reasonable levels for each website type.

Is free migration supported? __

Yes. AZDIGI supports free migration from old hosting to Unlimited Hosting. Technical team handles the entire process.

I'm using Unlimited 1, can I upgrade to Unlimited 3? __

Yes. You can flexibly upgrade to a higher plan within the same service at any time without needing to reconfigure or migrate data. The system will calculate the price difference multiplied by remaining days.
