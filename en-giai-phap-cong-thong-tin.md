## Frequently asked questions

Does AZDIGI design portals? __

AZDIGI is a hosting and VPS infrastructure provider. We don't design or develop portals, but provide powerful infrastructure for you or your development team to deploy portals.

Does a portal need hosting or VPS? __

Depends on scale: Small portals (<1000 users) can use Pro Platinum Hosting. Large portals or those requiring customization should use VPS for full admin access.

Does AZDIGI support CMS installation? __

Yes, our technical team supports free installation of Drupal, WordPress Multisite and other popular CMS on AZDIGI infrastructure.

How about backup and security? __

AZDIGI auto backups daily, 30-day retention. System includes Firewall, Anti-DDoS and free SSL to protect your portal.
