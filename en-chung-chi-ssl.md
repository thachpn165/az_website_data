## Frequently Asked Questions

How is free SSL (Let's Encrypt) different from paid SSL? __

Free SSL only has DV, no financial insurance, and needs renewal every 90 days. Paid SSL has insurance up to $1,000,000, OV/EV support, and longer validity.

How to know which SSL type my website needs? __

Blog/personal website: Sectigo PositiveSSL. Multiple subdomains: Sectigo PositiveSSL Wildcard. E-commerce, finance, banking: PositiveSSL EV.

Is free SSL installation supported? __

Yes. AZDIGI supports free SSL installation for all hosting/VPS at AZDIGI. Just contact technical team after purchasing SSL.

Does SSL affect SEO? __

Yes. Google confirms HTTPS is a ranking signal. Websites with SSL are prioritized in search results.

Can SSL be transferred to another server? __

Yes. You can reissue SSL and install on any server. Unlimited reissue count.

What does Wildcard SSL protect? __

Wildcard SSL protects main domain and all first-level subdomains, e.g.: domain.com, www.domain.com, blog.domain.com, shop.domain.com.
