## Frequently asked questions

Which VPS is suitable for SuiteCRM? __

SuiteCRM needs at least 2GB RAM. Recommend VPS from 4GB RAM for 5-20 users team, 8GB+ for larger teams.

Is self-hosted CRM difficult to manage? __

Not difficult. AZDIGI supports free installation and auto backup. You just use CRM like SaaS.

Can CRM integrate with WooCommerce? __

Yes, SuiteCRM and Odoo have plugins to integrate WooCommerce, Magento to sync customers and orders.
