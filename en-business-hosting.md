## Business Hosting FAQ

How is Business Hosting different from regular Shared Hosting? __

Business Hosting has very low customers per server, no resource overselling, uses enterprise infrastructure with NVMe RAID-10. Ensures stable performance for important business websites.

Should I choose Business Hosting or VPS? __

If you don't have server administration knowledge (Linux, command line), Business Hosting is a better choice. You get VPS-like performance but with easy cPanel, automatic backup, managed security and full technical support.

What's the difference between Premium Business and Turbo Business? __

Turbo Business has lower users/server, higher specs (6-22 vCPU, 45-200GB NVMe), supports LiteSpeed + Redis Cache. Suitable for e-commerce sites, projects needing highest performance.

What does low users/server mean? __

Instead of cramming hundreds of websites on same server like cheap hosting, Business Hosting only serves very limited customers. This ensures you always have enough CPU, RAM, I/O resources.

Is Business Hosting suitable for e-commerce websites? __

Very suitable. Business Hosting is optimized for e-commerce with LiteSpeed + Redis Cache, improving load speed and reducing database load.

Is free website migration supported? __

Yes. AZDIGI supports free website migration from old hosting. Technical team handles the entire process, ensuring continuous website operation without interruption.

Does Business Hosting include automatic backup? __

Yes. All Business Hosting plans include automatic daily backup, stored for 30 days. You can restore website anytime from cPanel.

Can I upgrade to VPS later? __

Yes. If your needs grow beyond Business Hosting, you can upgrade to VPS or Cloud Server. AZDIGI supports free migration with zero downtime.
