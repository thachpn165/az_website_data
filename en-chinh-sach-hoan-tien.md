## Refund Conditions
AZDIGI applies the following refund policy:

### Hosting
  * **First 30 days:** 100% refund
  * **After 30 days:** No refund

### VPS
  * **First 30 days:** 100% refund
  * **After 30 days:** No refund

### Domain
  * No refund after successful registration

### SSL
  * Certificate not issued: 100% refund
  * Certificate issued: No refund

2

## Request Deadline
To be considered for refund:

  * Request within the designated period for each service
  * Submit ticket with title: "Refund Request"
  * Provide reason for refund (optional for hosting 30 days)

**Note:** Requests after the deadline will not be accepted.

3

## Refund Process
  1. **Submit request:** Create support ticket
  2. **Verification:** AZDIGI checks conditions (1 - 3 business hours during office hours)
  3. **Approval:** Result notification via email
  4. **Execution:** Refund within 5-7 business days

### Required information:
  * Order ID / Invoice ID
  * Service name for refund
  * Bank account information (for cash refund)

4

## Non-Refundable Cases
AZDIGI does not refund in these cases:

  * Terms of service violation
  * Account suspended for violations
  * Past refund deadline
  * Successfully registered domains
  * Upgraded/downgraded services
  * One-time setup fees
  * Addon services

5

## Refund Methods
Refunds are processed via:

### 1\. Account Credit (Preferred)
  * Instant credit to AZDIGI account
  * Use for other services
  * No fees

### 2\. Bank Transfer
  * Time: 5-7 business days
  * Customer bears transfer fees (if any)

**Note:** Customers who paid via Apple Pay, VNPay, MoMo will receive refunds through the payment platform according to the platform provider's policies.
